## How It Works

Every Hoursback workflow follows the same four steps:

**1. Watch** — A trigger fires on a schedule (daily, weekly, monthly) or when data changes (new webhook, updated spreadsheet, website change).

**2. Fetch** — It pulls the latest data from wherever you keep it: a Google Sheet, a website, an API, a file upload, or an email you forwarded.

**3. Think** — Claude AI reads the data and performs the analysis. Not a generic summary — a focused, domain-specific answer to questions like: What changed? Why does it matter? What should I do next?

**4. Deliver** — A formatted report lands in your inbox with clear headings, bullet points, tables, and charts where relevant.

Set it up once. Get insights automatically, forever.

## What Makes It Different From Other Tools

**It's not Zapier or Make.com.**
Those tools connect apps together and automate tasks. Hoursback is about *reasoning* — taking your business data and producing intelligent, actionable analysis. It answers "what does this mean for my business?" not just "what happened?"

**It comes pre-built.**
You don't need to design workflows, write prompts, or understand AI. Every workflow is ready to deploy. Pick one, connect your data, set your schedule, and it runs.

**It gets better over time.**
Hoursback has a memory layer. It remembers your preferences (how you like reports formatted, what level of detail you want). It also remembers previous runs, so instead of just saying "competitor price is £39", it can say "competitor increased their price from £29 to £39 — this is the second increase in 3 months." Reports improve the longer you use it.

**It works with tools you already use.**
No new integrations. If your data lives in a Google Sheet, it reads that. If you have a website you want to monitor, it watches that. If you use webhooks from another system, it accepts those.

**It requires no technical knowledge.**
No code. No developers. No configuration beyond filling in a simple form. If you can use email, you can use Hoursback.
